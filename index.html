Our Story

Taqseem isn't just an organization – it's a dream come true. It all began with a heartfelt desire to give back to society, to empower individuals to stand tall and succeed. Imagine a world where the next generation grows up with passion, kindness, respect, and the ability to care for themselves and their community. This incredible journey took flight with a single photograph that struck a chord deep within Mr. Jahangir's heart. The image captured a father joyfully escorting his kids to school on a motorcycle, earning the title 'a real-life hero.' But it was the accompanying caption that truly resonated: "This picture means the world to me. My dad is my hero. He'd sit there with his newspaper, waiting to take me to school, even though it was just a minute away. Witnessing those kids brimming with excitement as they hopped onto the bike reminded me of my dad and all the unsung heroes we overlook every day."

The photographer behind that impactful image was none other than Mr. Jahangir's daughter, Iman Jahangir, the current CEO of Taqseem Foundation. The weight of the 'real hero' title spurred Mr. Jahangir into action. He knew he needed to do something tangible. And so, he shared his feelings with Iman, saying, "Every parent treasures the act of taking their kids to school. Let's pledge to help parents who can't afford to do the same. If you win, use the funds for this purpose." With that promise, the transformative journey began. Mr. Jahangir and his daughter, Iman, embarked on a mission to offer free education to children in need. Despite challenges, they held fast to their determination to make a meaningful impact. Today, after three years and even amidst a pandemic, Taqseem stands as a testament to their commitment. Four thriving schools, 1100 students, and countless lives touched by their unwavering dedication. Starting modestly with their own resources, they are gradually making education accessible to more and more individuals. Taqseem's story is one of chasing a dream, confronting challenges head-on, and believing wholeheartedly in the power of education. From a single photograph to four flourishing schools, it's a narrative that demonstrates the significance of turning intentions into actions, effecting genuine change. Taqseem isn't just about offering assistance – it's about transforming lives, one step at a time. And this journey is far from over; Taqseem continues to expand, spreading education and hope.

Mission

To empower less privileged children and women through education and enabling talent trainings, providing them with resources they need to enhance their skills to break the cycle of poverty and build a brighter future for themselves and their communities.

Vision

We are committed to supporting every child and woman within our capacity, providing access to education, nutritious meals and a nurturing environment to enable them to reach their full potential and contribute to a brighter future for all.

Values

Our organization's core values include empowering through education, ensuring inclusivity and integrity, practicing compassion, striving for excellence, fostering collaboration, respecting diversity, and prioritizing sustainability.

Our Future Aims 🎯

Taqseem Foundation aims to give every child a chance at education by offering free uniforms. This helps ensure equal opportunities and a sense of belonging for all students.

We strive to provide nourishment for young minds by giving free, nutritious lunches. A well-fed child is more likely to thrive academically and grow into a well-rounded individual.

Taqseem Foundation plans to broaden its academic spectrum by including more grades. This allows us to guide students through their educational journey, supporting their growth and development.

Our goal is to create and support more schools, reaching more deserving children. By doing so, we can extend quality education to more communities and elevate them socially and intellectually.

CEO & Co-Founder

Jahangir Waheed, the dynamic founder chairman of Taqseem Foundation, manifests a vision of societal betterment through philanthropy. Ambreen Waheed

Director

Ambreen Waheed, Director of Taqseem Foundation and accomplished global leader in Responsible Business. Renowned for pioneering work on ethical practices, anti-corruption, and sustainable development. She led CONCEPT TO IMPLEMENTATION 6 , LSK-(Learning Skills for Kids) Schools around Lahore to move children from waste sorting within Corporate Supply Chain, to gain healthy Life, respect, and education. Ambreen holds an MPhil from the University of Cambridge and has extensive experience advising global organizations and imparting knowledge to International universities such as Wharton Business USA and Essec Business France.
